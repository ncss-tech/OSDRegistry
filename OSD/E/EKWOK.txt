Landscape--plains

Slope--0 to 70 percent

Mean annual precipitation--about 800 mm

Depth class--very deep

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, isotic Typic Haplocryods

TYPICAL PEDON: Ekwok silt loam on a rise of a plain, on a 3-percent slope at an elevation of 31 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on July 31, 2006.)

 Oi--0 to 3 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; many very fine, fine, and medium and few coarse roots; moderately acid (pH 5.8); clear smooth boundary

 E/A--3 to 7 cm; ashy silt loam, matrix color 60 percent light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) moist, and 40 percent brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; many very fine and fine, common medium, and few coarse roots; extremely acid (pH 4.4); clear smooth boundary

 Bhs--7 to 15 cm; medial silt loam, reddish brown (5YR 5/4) dry, dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and medium and few coarse roots; very strongly acid (pH 4.8); abrupt wavy boundary

 2Bs--15 to 50 cm; silt loam, matrix color 75 percent light brown (7.5YR 6/4) dry, brown (7.5YR 4/4) moist, and 25 percent brown (7.5YR 5/2) dry, dark brown (7.5YR 3/2) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine and few medium and coarse roots; moderately acid (pH 5.6); clear wavy boundary

 2C--50 to 180 cm; sandy loam, light brown (7.5YR 6/4) dry, brown (7.5YR 4/4) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and medium and few coarse roots; 5 percent fine masses of oxidized iron, strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist, diffuse boundaries; 25 percent mottles, light gray (2.5Y 7/2) dry, grayish brown (2.5Y 5/2) moist; moderately acid (pH 5.6)

TYPE LOCATION: Dillingham Census Area, Alaska, village of Ekwok, on the Nushagak River; about 70 km northeast of Dillingham, Alaska; latitude 59.358611 degrees north, longitude 157.473889 degrees west, datum WGS 84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
