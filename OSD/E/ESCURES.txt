Mean annual precipitation--about 2600 mm

Depth class--moderately deep

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Loamy-skeletal, mixed, superactive, nonacid Lithic Cryorthents

TYPICAL PEDON: Escures sandy loam on a mountain escarpment, on a slope of 80 percent at an elevation of 115 m (When described on September 1, 2013, the soil was moist throughout.)

 Oe--0 to 3 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; moderately acid (pH 6.0); abrupt wavy boundary

 A--3 to 10 cm; gravelly coarse sandy loam, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; moderate fine granular structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and coarse and many medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 5 percent cobbles, 10 percent medium gravel, and 10 percent fine gravel; slightly acid (pH 6.4); clear wavy boundary

 CA--10 to 25 cm; very cobbly coarse sandy loam, brownish yellow (10YR 6/6) dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6) moist; massive; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 5 percent cobbles, 10 percent fine gravel, and 25 percent medium gravel; neutral (pH 7.2); clear wavy boundary

 C--25 to 54 cm; very cobbly coarse sandy loam, light gray (2.5Y 7/1) dry, gray (2.5Y 5/1) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; few very fine and fine interstitial pores; 5 percent cobbles, 15 percent fine gravel, and 25 percent medium gravel; slightly alkaline (pH 7.8); abrupt irregular boundary

 2R--54 cm; bedrock

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area; latitude 58.6055116, longitude -136.4561523, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
