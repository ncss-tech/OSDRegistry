KOUWEGOK SERIES

Landscape--coastal plain
Landform--floodplains, leeward side of coastal berms
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--loamy alluvium over sandy and gravelly alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 300 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -3 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy over sandy or sandy-skeletal, isotic over mixed Cumulic Haplocryolls

TYPICAL PEDON: Kouwegok stratified sandy loam on a linear, linear floodplain, on the leeward side of a coastal berm, on a slope of 0 percent at an elevation of 3 m (The soil was moist throughout the profile when described on July 12, 2015.)

 Oe--0 to 5 cm; moderately decomposed plant material, brown (7.5YR 5/2) dry, dark brown (7.5YR 3/2) moist; many very fine roots and few medium roots and common fine roots; neutral (pH 7.0); clear smooth boundary

 A1--5 to 12 cm; stratified sandy loam, 70 percent grayish brown (10YR 5/2) and 30 percent dark gray (10YR 4/1) dry, 70 percent very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) and 30 percent black (10YR 2/1) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and few fine roots; few fine irregular pores; slightly acid (pH 6.4); clear smooth boundary

 A2--12 to 22 cm; stratified silt loam, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak thin platy structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine roots; slightly acid (pH 6.4); clear smooth boundary

 A3--22 to 49 cm; stratified silt loam, 40 percent brown (7.5YR 4/3) and 40 percent brown (7.5YR 4/4) and 20 percent gray (7.5YR 5/1) dry, 40 percent black (7.5YR 2.5/1) and 40 percent very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) and 20 percent very dark gray (7.5YR 3/1) moist; moderate coarse subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine roots; strongly acid (pH 5.4); clear smooth boundary

 AB--49 to 80 cm; stratified silt loam, brown (7.5YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (7.5YR 3/3) moist; moderate coarse subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; few very fine roots; strongly acid (pH 5.4); gradual smooth boundary

 2CA--80 to 150 cm; extremely channery loamy sand, brown (7.5YR 5/3) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/3) moist; massive; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; 65 percent channers; moderately acid (pH 5.6)
