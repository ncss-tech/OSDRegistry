MAREJADA SERIES

MLRA(s)--273
Depth Class--Very deep
Drainage Class--Subaqueous drainage
Water depth range--0 to 200 centimeters
Saturated hydraulic conductivity--Moderately high to high
Parent Material--Fine-loamy estuarine deposits over mangrove organic materials
Geomorphic location--Submerged mangrove swamp
Slope--0 to 1 percent
Mean Annual Air Temperature--26 degrees C (79 degrees F)
Mean Annual Precipitation--1041 millimeters (41 inches)
Mean Annual Water Temperature--28 degrees C (82 degrees F)

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Fine-loamy, mixed, subactive, nonacid, isohyperthermic Thapto-histic Hydrowassents

TYPICAL PEDON: Marejada muck in a submerged wave-built terrace in a coastal lagoon.  (Colors are for moist soil unless otherwise stated)

 Aseg--0 to 9 centimeters; very dark greenish gray (5GY 3/1), silty clay loam; structureless, massive; non-sticky, nonplastic, very fluid; slight sulfurous odor; color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderate effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), slightly alkaline (pH 7.4) oxidized pH; clear boundary.

 Cseg1--9 to 21 centimeters; dark greenish gray (10GY 4/1), shelly silty clay loam; 25 percent shell fragments; structureless, massive; slightly sticky, nonplastic, slightly fluid; slight sulfurous odor; no color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderate effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), slightly alkaline (pH 7.8) oxidized pH; clear boundary.

 Cseg2--21 to 31 centimeters; dark greenish gray (10GY 4/1), silty clay; structureless, massive; slightly sticky, slightly plastic, slightly fluid; no sulfurous odor; no color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide; slight effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), slightly alkaline (pH 7.8) oxidized pH; clear boundary.

 Cseg3--31 to 44 centimeters; 75 percent greenish black (5GY 2.5/1) and 25 percent very dark gray (5Y 3/1), mucky shelly loam; 20 percent shell fragments; structureless, massive; nonsticky, nonplastic, slightly fluid; slight sulfurous odor; color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide, slight effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), slightly alkaline (pH 7.4) oxidized pH; abrupt boundary.

 2Oaseb1--44 to 78 centimeters; very dark brown (10YR 2/2), muck; 20 percent unrubbed fiber, 5 percent rubbed fiber, 2 percent mineral; H7 von Post; 20 percent wood fragments (red mangrove); structureless, massive; strong sulfurous odor;  color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide, slight effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), very strongly acid (pH 5.0) oxidized pH; clear boundary.

 2Oaseb2--78 to 115 centimeters; very dark brown (10YR 2/2), muck; 20 percent unrubbed fiber, 5 percent rubbed fiber, 10 percent mineral; H9 von Post; structureless, massive; strong sulfurous odor;  color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide, slight effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), very strongly acid (pH 4.6) oxidized pH; abrupt boundary.

 2Cseg1--115 to 134 centimeters; very dark greenish gray (10GY 3/1), sandy loam; 2 percent shell fragments; structureless, massive; nonsticky, nonplastic, slightly fluid; strong sulfurous odor; no color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide; slight effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), very strongly acid (pH 4.6) oxidized pH; abrupt boundary.

 2Cseg2--134 to 203 centimeters; 50 percent very dark greenish gray (10Y3/1), 40 percent white 10YR 8/1 (coral sand grains), and 10 percent dark greenish gray (5GY 4/1), coarse sand; 2 percent shell fragments; structureless, single grain; nonsticky, nonplastic, nonfluid; slight sulfurous odor; no color change with 3 percent hydrogen peroxide; no effervescence with 30 percent hydrogen peroxide; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0), very strongly acid (pH 4.7) oxidized pH.

TYPE LOCATION: Guayama municipality, Puerto Rico. Approximately 2.2 kilometers (1.34 miles) east of Jobos Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve dock and 1.6 kilometers (1.0 mile) north-northeast of the Nautical Club at Punta Pozuelo Guayama in submerged former mangrove swamp parallel to the beach south of San Felipe and Mosquito communities.

GEOGRAPHIC SETTING:
Parent Material--Fine loamy estuarine deposits over mangrove organic materials
Landscape--Coastal lagoons in Semiarid Coastal Plain, southern Puerto Rico
Landform--Submerged mangrove swamp
Mean Annual Air Temperature--22 to 31 degrees C (72 to 88 degrees F)
Mean Annual Precipitation--508 to 1,145 millimeters (20 to 45 inches)
Mean Annual Water Temperature--26 to 32 degrees C (79 to 90 degrees F)
Water salinity range--28 to 43 parts per thousand

GEOGRAPHICALLY ASSOCIATED SOILS:
These are the Mayaguas and Ostiones soils.
Majaguas soils--are on lagoon bottoms and can occur on drains on the mainland cove. They do not have buried organic soil materials but also have common fine-loamy particle size.
Ostiones soils--are on submerged mainland beaches on the mainland cove occurring around and slightly higher on the landscape to Marejada. They have coarser textures throughout and do not have buried organic soil materials

DRAINAGE AND SATURATED HYDRAULIC CONDUCTIVITY:
Drainage class--Subaqueous (permanently submersed)
Saturated hydraulic conductivity--Moderately high to high
Soil moisture regime--Peraquic
Soil is continuously inundated by saline and brackish water

USE AND VEGETATION:
Major Uses--Used for wildlife habitat, recreational boating, fishing, and swimming, marinas, mooring fields, and commercial shellfish habitat.  These soils provide improved water quality and flood / storm surge water storage. Dominant Vegetation--Common subaquatic vegetation (SAV) species include Thalassia (Turtle grass), Syringodium (manatee grass), halimeda (watercress algae), and Halofila(paddle grass) Halimeda opuntia).

DISTRIBUTION AND EXTENT: 
General area--Located along coasts and bays of Puerto Rico.
Land Resource Region--Z - Caribbean Region
MLRA--273 - Semiarid Coastal Plains
