Landscape--alluvial plains

Slope--0 to 3 percent
Parent material--organic material over alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 255 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -6 degrees C
Depth class--very deep

Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--gelic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-silty, mixed, superactive, calcareous, subgelic Fluvaquentic Aquorthels

TYPICAL PEDON: Chissovun mucky peat on a woodland terrace with a slope of 2 percent and an elevation of 130 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on September 13, 2015.)

 Oe--0 to 11 cm; mucky peat; dark reddish brown (5YR 2.5/2) moist; many very fine roots, and common fine to very coarse roots; very strongly acid (pH 5.0); clear smooth boundary

 Oa--11 to 22 cm; muck; black (5YR 2.5/1) moist; common very fine to very coarse roots; strongly acid (pH 5.5); clear irregular boundary

 Cg--22 to 38 cm; silt loam; 50 percent gray (2.5Y 6/1) dry, dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) moist, and 50 percent light gray (2.5Y 7/1) dry, gray (2.5Y 5/1) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine and fine roots; few fine and medium pores; 30 percent strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist, masses of oxidized iron in matrix; noneffervescent; neutral (pH 7.2); clear wavy boundary

 Cg/Oajj--38 to 67 cm; silt loam; 90 percent light brownish gray (2.5Y 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (2.5Y 4/2) moist, and 10 percent dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and nonplastic; common fine roots; few fine and medium pores; 10 percent strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist, masses of oxidized iron in matrix; slightly effervescent; slightly alkaline (pH 7.5); abrupt wavy boundary

 Cgf/Oajjf--67 to 100 cm; silt loam; 90 percent light brownish gray (2.5Y 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (2.5Y 4/2) moist, and 10 percent dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; massive; hard, very firm, slightly sticky and nonplastic; 7 percent strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist, masses of oxidized iron in matrix; slightly effervescent; slightly alkaline (pH 7.6)
