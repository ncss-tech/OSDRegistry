MUDIE SERIES

Landscape--shore complexes
Landform--tidal flats
Slope--0 to 3 percent
Parent material--marine deposits
Mean annual precipitation--about 600 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 1 degree C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--very poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--peraquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-silty, isotic, nonacid Typic Cryaquents

TYPICAL PEDON: Mudie silt loam on a linear tidal flat, on a slope of 1 percent at an elevation of 3 m (The soil was saturated throughout the profile when described on September 18, 2007.)

 ACg--0 to 13 cm; silt loam, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, dark gray (5Y 4/1) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; soft, friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; many very fine, fine, and medium roots; neutral (pH 7.3); gradual smooth boundary

 Cg1--13 to 29 cm; silt loam, light gray (2.5Y 7/1) dry, gray (2.5Y 5/1) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots; 25 percent medium masses of oxidized iron, strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist, clear boundaries; neutral (pH 6.9); gradual smooth boundary

 Cg2--29 to 60 cm; silt loam, gray (2.5Y6/1) dry, dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; 45 percent medium masses of oxidized iron, yellowish red (5YR 4/6) moist, sharp boundaries; neutral (pH 6.8); gradual smooth boundary

 Cg3--60 to 180 cm; silt loam, gray (N 5/0) dry, very dark gray (N 3/0) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and slightly plastic; neutral (pH 6.9)

TYPE LOCATION: Dillingham Census Area, Alaska, in the village of Ekuk; latitude 58.813056 degrees north, longitude 158.5547222 degrees west, datum WGS 84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
