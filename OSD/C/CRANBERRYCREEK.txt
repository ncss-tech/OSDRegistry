CRANBERRYCREEK SERIES

Landscape--plains, hills
Landform--rises, hillslopes
Slope--0 to 18 percent
Parent material--loess with drift in lower part of some pedons
Mean annual precipitation--about 900 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 0 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, isotic Spodic Dystrocryepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Cranberrycreek silt loam on a northwest-facing, convex rise of a plain, on a slope of 3 percent at an elevation of 85 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on September 14, 2010.)

 Oe--0 to 3 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; common very fine to medium roots; extremely acid (pH 3.6); clear wavy boundary

 A--3 to 10 cm; silt loam, brown (7.5YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) moist; weak fine granular structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; extremely acid (pH 4.0); clear wavy boundary

 Bhs--10 to 15 cm; medial silt loam, reddish brown (5YR 5/4) dry, dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) moist; weak fine granular structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; extremely acid (pH 4.0); abrupt wavy boundary

 Bw/Ajj--15 to 66 cm; silt loam, matrix color 75 percent pale yellow (2.5Y 7/3) dry, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) moist, 20 percent pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, brown (10YR 4/3) moist, and 5 percent grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak medium granular structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; 3 percent medium masses of oxidized iron, strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist, clear boundaries; very strongly acid (pH 4.6); clear wavy boundary 

 BC--66 to 160 cm; very fine sandy loam, pale yellow (2.5Y 7/3) dry, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) moist; weak medium granular structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; 15 percent medium masses of oxidized iron, dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6) moist, clear boundaries; very strongly acid (pH 4.6); gradual wavy boundary

 C--160 to 180 cm; silt loam, pale yellow (2.5Y 7/3) dry, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) dry; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; 10 percent medium masses of oxidized iron, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/6) moist, clear boundaries; very strongly acid (pH 4.7)
TYPE LOCATION: Dillingham Census Area, Alaska, about 95 km northeast of the city of Dillingham; latitude 59.6775000 degrees north, longitude 157.3966667 degrees west, datum WGS 84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
