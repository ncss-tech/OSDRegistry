DEADHORSE SERIES

Landscape--hills, alluvial plains
Landform--fan remnants
Slope--1 to 50 percent
Parent material--loess-influenced alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 350 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--moderately deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--xeric
Soil temperature regime--frigid

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Loamy-skeletal, mixed, superactive, frigid Typic Durixerolls

TYPICAL PEDON: Deadhorse silt loam in an area of rangeland (Colors are for dry soil unless otherwise noted.)

 A1--0 to 10 cm; dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) silt loam, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure parting to moderate medium granular; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common fine and very fine and few medium roots; common very fine tubular pores; 10 percent gravel; slightly alkaline (pH 7.8); clear wavy boundary

 Bt--10 to 20 cm; grayish brown (10YR 5/2) gravelly silt loam, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak medium and fine subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, firm, slightly sticky and plastic; common fine and very fine and few medium roots; common very fine tubular pores; 30 percent thin clay films on ped faces and in pores; 25 percent gravel; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); abrupt wavy boundary

 Btk--20 to 33 cm; brown (10YR 5/3) very gravelly silt loam, brown (10YR 4/3) moist; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, firm, slightly sticky and plastic; common fine and very fine and few medium roots; common fine and very fine tubular pores; 15 percent thin clay films on ped faces; 45 percent gravel; strongly effervescent (17 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); abrupt wavy boundary

 Bk--33 to 53 cm; very pale brown (10YR 8/2) extremely gravelly loam, yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) moist; massive; slightly hard, firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few fine and very fine roots; few very fine tubular pores; finely disseminated calcium carbonates; 60 percent gravel and 10 percent cobbles; violently effervescent (50 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.2); abrupt wavy boundary

 2Bkqm--53 to 97 cm; very pale brown (10YR 8/2) indurated duripan, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) moist; massive; extremely hard, extremely firm; violently effervescent; 40 percent gravel; strongly alkaline (pH 8.6); abrupt wavy boundary

 3Bk--97 to 152 cm; very pale brown (10YR 8/4) very gravelly loamy sand, very pale brown (10YR 7/4) moist; massive; soft, very friable; finely disseminated carbonates; violently effervescent; 40 percent gravel and 5 percent cobbles; strongly alkaline (pH 8.8)
