Landscape--hills, mountains
Landform--hillslopes, mountain slopes
Slope--1 to 45 percent
Parent material--loess and colluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 550 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -3 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Loamy-skeletal, mixed, superactive Typic Dystrocryepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Iiguk silt loam on a west facing convex, convex summit on a slope of 8 percent at an elevation of 237 m (The soil was moist throughout the profile when described on June 28, 2016.)

 Oa--0 to 7 cm; highly decomposed plant material, brown (7.5YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) moist; common very fine and fine roots; very strongly acid (pH 4.6); clear wavy boundary

 A--7 to 15 cm; very gravelly silt loam, brown (7.5YR 4/3) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/3) moist; weak medium subangular blocky parts to moderate fine granular structure; very friable; common very fine, fine, and medium roots; few fine irregular pores; 5 percent cobbles, 10 percent channers, 20 percent gravel; very strongly acid (pH 4.8); clear wavy boundary

 Bw/Ajj--15 to 53 cm; very gravelly silt loam, 90 percent light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) and 10 percent dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry, 90 percent dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) and 10 percent very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; weak coarse subangular blocky parts to weak fine granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots; few fine irregular pores; 10 percent cobbles, 10 percent channers, 30 percent gravel; very strongly acid (pH 4.8); clear smooth boundary

 C--53 to 150 cm; very gravelly silt loam, light yellowish brown (2.5Y 6/4) dry, olive brown (2.5Y 4/4) moist; massive; friable, nonsticky, slightly plastic; 10 percent fine faint dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) mottles and 10 percent fine distinct strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; 10 percent cobbles, 20 percent channers, 20 percent gravel; extremely acid (pH 4.4)
