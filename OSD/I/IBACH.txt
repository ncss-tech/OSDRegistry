IBACH SERIES

Landscape--till plain
Landform--moraines
Slope--2 to 35 percent
Parent material--till
Mean annual precipitation--about 1400 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Loamy-skeletal, mixed, superactive Typic Humicryepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Ibach silt loam on a moraine, on a slope of 3 percent at an elevation of 39 m (When described on August 01, 2014, the soil was moist throughout.)

 Oi--0 to 9 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; extremely acid (pH 4.4), gradual broken boundary

 A--9 to 27 cm; bouldery silt loam, brown (10YR 4/3) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak medium granular structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and very coarse and many fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; many very fine and fine irregular pores; 1 percent sedimentary flagstones, 3 percent cobbles, 3 percent diorite stones, 5 percent diorite gravel, and 20 percent diorite boulders; extremely acid (pH 3.8); gradual broken boundary

 C1--27 to 56 cm; extremely bouldery sandy loam, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/4) dry, olive brown (2.5Y 4/3) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, nonsticky and nonplastic; common medium roots throughout; many very fine and fine irregular pores; 5 percent fine diorite gravel, 7 percent cobbles, 10 percent diorite stones, and 65 percent diorite boulders; strongly acid (pH 5.4); gradual wavy boundary

 C2--56 to 73 cm; extremely gravelly sandy loam, light yellowish brown (2.5Y 6/4) dry, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 1 percent stones, 10 percent cobbles, and 65 percent diorite gravel; strongly acid (pH 5.4); gradual wavy boundary

 C3--73 to 150 cm; extremely gravelly sandy loam, light yellowish brown (2.5Y 6/4) dry, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine and fine irregular pores; 2 percent diorite stones, 10 percent diorite cobbles, and 55 percent medium diorite gravel; moderately acid (pH 5.8)

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area; latitude 58.4508537, longitude -135.8887977, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
