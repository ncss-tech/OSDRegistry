Landscape--valleys
Landform--floodplain
Slope--0 to 10 percent
Parent material--loamy alluvium over sandy and gravelly alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 485 mm

Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--somewhat poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy over sandy or sandy-skeletal, mixed, superactive, nonacid Fluvaquentic Cryaquepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Swiftriver silt on a linear, linear floodplain on a slope of 0 percent at an elevation of 93 m (The soil was wet, satiated, from 68 to 150 cm and moist from 0 to 68 cm when described on July 19, 2016.)

 A1--0 to 6 cm; silt, gray (7.5YR 5/1) dry, very dark gray (7.5YR 3/1) moist; weak, fine, granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine and medium, and many fine roots; neutral (pH 6.9); clear irregular boundary

 A2--6 to 25 cm; silt, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak, medium, granular structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine, medium, and fine roots; neutral (pH 6.7); clear smooth boundary

 Bg--25 to 44 cm; silt and fine sandy loam, dark gray (N 4/), dry, black (N 2.5/), moist; weak, coarse, subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; common medium tubular pores; 5 percent medium irregular dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron with clear boundaries on surfaces along root channels and 5 percent medium irregular dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron with clear boundaries lining pores and 5 percent medium irregular dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron with clear boundaries in matrix; neutral (pH 6.6); abrupt irregular boundary

 2Cg--44 to 150 cm; extremely gravely sand, gray (2.5Y 5/1) dry, very dark gray (2.5Y 3/1) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky, nonplastic; 10 percent cobbles, 50 percent gravel; neutral (pH 6.7)
