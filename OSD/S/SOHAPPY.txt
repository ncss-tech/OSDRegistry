SOHAPPY SERIES

Landscape--plateaus
Landform--structural benches
Slope--2 to 60 percent
Parent material--loess over colluvium derived from basalt and interbedded sedimentary material
Mean annual precipitation--about 200 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 10 degrees C
Depth class--deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--aridic
Soil temperature regime--mesic
Soil moisture subclass--xeric

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, mixed, superactive, mesic Duric Xeric Haplocalcids

TYPICAL PEDON: Sohappy silt loam in an area of rangeland, on a 3-percent slope at an elevation of 550 m

 A--0 to 15 cm; silt loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; moderate thin and medium platy structure parting to weak fine subangular blocky; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine and medium and few coarse roots; many very fine and fine irregular pores; neutral (pH 7.0); clear smooth boundary

 Bw1--15 to 46 cm; silt loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak medium prismatic structure parting to weak medium subangular blocky; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine and medium and few coarse roots; many very fine and fine irregular pores; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); clear smooth boundary

 Bw2--46 to 86 cm; silt loam, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry, brown (10YR 4/3) moist; weak medium prismatic structure parting to weak medium subangular blocky; slightly hard, firm, nonsticky and nonplastic; few fine, medium, and coarse roots; many very fine and fine irregular pores; 5 percent basalt gravel; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); abrupt wavy boundary

 2Bk--86 to 114 cm; gravelly fine sandy loam, light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) dry, brown (10YR 4/3) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine roots, matted on pan fragments at a depth of 114 cm; common fine irregular pores; 10 percent basalt gravel and 5 percent basalt cobbles; strongly effervescent; abrupt smooth boundary

 2Bkqm1--114 to 132 cm; fractured lime-silica pan; 20 percent gravel and 5 percent cobbles; clear smooth boundary

 2Bkqm2--132 cm; indurated lime-silica duripan

TYPE LOCATION: Yakima County, Washington, about 19 km south of Toppenish, off Sohappy Road; about 30 m north and 90 m west of the southeast corner of section 7, T. 8 N., R. 20 E.; latitude 46.1859360, longitude -120.3467636, datum WGS 84 (coordinates estimated from section, township, and range)
