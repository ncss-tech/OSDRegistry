Landscape--hills, mountains

Slope--0 to 50 percent
Parent material--colluvium, residuum
Mean annual precipitation--about 1000 mm

Soil temperature regime--cryic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Loamy-skeletal, isotic Typic Dystrocryepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Sharp silt loam on a northeast-facing, convex shoulder on the center third of a mountain, on a slope of 12 percent at an elevation of 442 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on July 31, 2008.)

 Oe--0 to 2 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; many very fine, common fine, and few medium roots; very strongly acid (pH 4.6); clear smooth boundary

 A--2 to 6 cm; silt loam, brown (7.5YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) moist; weak fine granular structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine to medium roots; 10 percent gravel; very strongly acid (pH 4.9); clear wavy boundary

 Bw1--6 to 28 cm; gravelly silt loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, brown (10YR 4/3) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine to medium roots; 25 percent gravel and 5 percent cobbles; strongly acid (pH 5.4); clear wavy boundary

 Bw2--28 to 69 cm; very gravelly silt loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine to medium roots; 40 percent gravel and 15 percent cobbles; slightly acid (pH 6.1); gradual wavy boundary

 Bw3--69 to 140 cm; extremely gravelly silt loam, yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine and fine roots; 25 percent gravel, 25 percent cobbles, and 20 percent stones; moderately acid (pH 6.0); very abrupt irregular boundary

 2R--140 cm; bedrock

TYPE LOCATION: Dillingham Census Area, Alaska, about 150 km northeast of the city of Dillingham; latitude 59.8188889 degrees north, longitude 156.3266667 degrees west, datum WGS 84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
