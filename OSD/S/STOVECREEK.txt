STOVECREEK SERIES

Landscape--mountains, hills
Landform--drainageways
Slope--0 to 10 percent
Parent material--coarse-loamy alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 505 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -3 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, isotic, nonacid Humic Cryaquepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Stovecreek peaty silt loam on an east southeast facing linear, linear floodplain on a slope of 6 percent at an elevation of 181 m (The soil was wet, non-satiated, from 0 to 29 cm and wet, satiated, from 23 to 150 cm when described on June 4, 2018.)

 A1--0 to 2 cm; peaty silt loam, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; moderate fine granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and common fine roots; moderately acid (pH 6.0); abrupt smooth boundary

 A2--2 to 15 cm; silt loam, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; moderate fine granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and common fine, medium and coarse roots; moderately acid (pH 5.9); clear smooth boundary

 A3--15 to 45 cm; silt loam, grayish brown (2.5Y 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (2.5Y 3/2) moist; moderate fine granular structure; friable, nonsticky, slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots; 10 percent medium faint dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) masses of oxidized iron in matrix; slightly acid (pH 6.1); gradual smooth boundary

 Cg1--45 to 90 cm; silt loam, dark greenish gray (10Y 4/1) dry, greenish black (10Y 2.5/1) moist; massive; friable, slightly sticky, slightly plastic; few fine irregular pores; 5 percent medium faint dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) masses of oxidized iron in matrix; slightly acid (pH 6.2); gradual smooth boundary

 Cg2--90 to 150 cm; loam, dark greenish gray (10Y 4/1) dry, greenish black (10Y 2.5/1) moist; massive; very friable, slightly sticky, nonplastic; few fine irregular pores; 10 percent by volume; slightly acid (pH 6.2)

TYPE LOCATION: Nome Census Area, Alaska, latitude 63.7186059 longitude -160.4271030 degrees, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined with a GPS unit)
