Landscape--valleys

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, isotic, acid, subgelic Histic Gelaquepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Jacksoncreek peat on an east facing convex, convex terrace on a slope of 3 percent at an elevation of 148 m (The soil was moist from 0 to 19 cm, wet from 39 to 150 cm and wet, non-satiated, from 19 to 39 cm when described on June 11, 2019.)

 Oi--0 to 17 cm; peat, yellow (10YR 7/6) dry, yellowish brown (10YR 5/6) moist; nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; many very fine, fine, and medium roots throughout; extremely acid (pH 3.8); clear smooth boundary

 Oe--17 to 26 cm; mucky peat, brown (7.5YR 4/3) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/3) moist; nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; common very fine, few medium, and many fine roots throughout; extremely acid (pH 4.2); clear wavy boundary

 Bgjj--26 to 45 cm; very fine sandy loam, 95 percent light gray (5Y 7/1) and 5 percent brown (7.5YR 4/2) dry, 95 percent dark greenish gray (10Y 4/1) and 5 percent very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) moist; weak coarse subangular blocky structure; firm, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; common very fine and few medium roots throughout; very fine irregular pores; 15 percent medium lenticular strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; very strongly acid (pH 4.8); clear irregular boundary

 Bg--45 to 110 cm; very fine sandy loam, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, dark greenish gray (10Y 4/1) moist; moderate medium platy parts to moderate medium granular structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; very fine irregular pores; 25 percent medium lenticular dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; very strongly acid (pH 5.0); clear smooth boundary

 C--110 to 128 cm; very fine sandy loam, olive gray (5Y 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (2.5Y 3/2) moist; massive; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; very fine irregular pores; 5 percent fine irregular dark gray (5Y 4/1) iron depletions and 10 percent fine irregular strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; strongly acid (pH 5.2); clear smooth boundary

 Cg--128 to 150 cm; very fine sandy loam, gray (5Y 5/1) dry, dark greenish gray (10Y 3/1) moist; massive; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; very fine irregular pores; 5 percent fine irregular strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; very strongly acid (pH 5.0)
