TYPICAL PEDON: Benjamincreek silt on a linear, linear terrace tread on a slope of 0 percent at an elevation of 92 m (The soil was moist from 0 to 55 cm, wet, non-satiated, from 55 to 65 cm, and moist from 65 to 150 cm when described on June 24, 2016.)

 Oi--0 to 17 cm; slightly decomposed plant material, brown (7.5YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (7.5YR 3/3) moist; nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine, medium, and coarse and many medium roots; extremely acid (pH 3.9); clear smooth boundary

 Oe--17 to 22 cm; moderately decomposed plant material, pinkish gray (7.5YR 6/2) dry, brown (7.5YR 4/2) moist; nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots; extremely acid (pH 3.9); abrupt smooth boundary

 Ajj--22 to 35 cm; silt, 75 percent brown (7.5YR 4/2) and 25 percent dark gray (10YR 4/1) dry, 75 percent very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) and 25 percent black (10YR 2/1) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots; very strongly acid (pH 4.8); clear smooth boundary

 Bhs--35 to 50 cm; highly organic silt loam, 60 percent dark reddish gray (5YR 4/2) and 40 percent grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, 60 percent dark reddish brown (5YR 2.5/2) and 40 percent very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak coarse subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine roots; 5 percent medium irregular dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron with diffuse boundaries in matrix; strongly acid (pH 5.2); clear smooth boundary

 C/Ajjg--50 to 85 cm; silt loam, light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) moist; few medium tubular and few fine irregular pores; massive; firm, nonsticky, nonplastic; 5 percent fine irregular gray (5Y 5/1) iron depletions with clear boundaries in matrix and 5 percent fine irregular strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron with clear boundaries in matrix; strongly acid (pH 5.3); diffuse smooth boundary

 2C--85 to 150 cm; silt loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, brown (10YR 4/3) moist; massive; firm, nonsticky, nonplastic; strongly acid (pH 5.4)
