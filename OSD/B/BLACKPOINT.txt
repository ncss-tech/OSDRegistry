BLACKPOINT SERIES

Landscape--hills and mountains
Landform--hillslopes and mountain slopes
Slope--20 to 70 percent
Parent material--colluvium over residuum
Mean annual precipitation--about 300 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -3 degrees C
Depth class--moderately deep to paralithic bedrock
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, isotic Typic Dystrocryepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Blackpoint silt loam on a south facing convex, linear backslope, on a slope of 55 percent at an elevation of 237 m (The soil was dry to 30 cm and moist throughout the rest of the profile when described on July 18, 2019.)

 Oe--0 to 2 cm; moderately decomposed plant material, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) dry, black (10YR 2/1) moist; many very fine, fine, and medium roots; very strongly acid (pH 4.9); abrupt irregular boundary

 A--2 to 8 cm; silt loam, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; weak very fine granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and fine roots; few very fine, fine, and medium irregular pores; very strongly acid (pH 5.0); abrupt irregular boundary

 Bw1--8 to 17 cm; silt loam, dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and common fine roots; few very fin, fine, and medium irregular pores; 10 percent angular gravel; strongly acid (pH 5.3); clear irregular boundary

 Bw2--17 to 32 cm; gravelly silt loam, brown (7.5YR 4/4) dry, very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) moist; weak coarse granular structure; very friable, slightly sticky, moderately plastic; many very fine and few fine roots; common very fine irregular pores; 25 percent angular gravel; strongly acid (pH 5.3); clear smooth boundary

 Bw3--32 to 85 cm; gravelly silt loam, 50 percent light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) and 50 percent pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, 50 percent dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) and 50 percent brown (10YR 4/3) moist; moderate thin platy structure; friable, slightly sticky, nonplastic; few very fine and fine roots; many very fine irregular pores; 3 percent medium dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6), moist, masses of oxidized iron; 25 percent angular gravel; strongly acid (pH 5.4); gradual smooth boundary

 Cr--85 cm; dark gray (5Y 4/1) dry, black (5Y 2/1) moist
