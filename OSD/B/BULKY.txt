BULKY SERIES

Landscape--outwash plains
Landform--outwash deltas
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--outwash
Mean annual precipitation--about 2500 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--very poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Sandy, mixed Typic Cryaquepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Bulky sandy loam, forested, on an outwash delta, on a slope of 1 percent at an elevation of 7 m (When described on June 21, 2014, the soil was wet throughout.)

 Oe--0 to 5 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; extremely acid (pH 4.2); clear wavy boundary

 Bg1--5 to 25 cm; sandy loam, dark gray (5Y 4/1) dry, dark bluish gray (5B 4/1) moist; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and, medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 20 percent masses of oxidized iron, strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist; strongly acid (pH 5.4); abrupt wavy boundary

 Bg2--25 to 50 cm; silt loam, dark gray (5Y 4/1) dry, dark greenish gray (5G 4/1) moist; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine roots; common very fine interstitial pores; moderately acid (pH 6.0); clear smooth boundary

 Cg--50 to 150 cm; sand, dark gray (5Y 4/1) dry, dark gray (N 4/0) moist; massive; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; very slightly effervescent (HCL, 1N); neutral (pH 6.9)

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area; latitude 58.3762680, longitude -136.3793060, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
