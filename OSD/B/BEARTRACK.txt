BEARTRACK SERIES

Landscape--shore complexes
Landform--fluviomarine bottoms
Slope--0 to 3 percent
Parent material--silty estuarine deposits
Mean annual precipitation--about 1420 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-silty, mixed, superactive, calcareous Typic Cryaquents

TYPICAL PEDON: Beartrack silt loam on a fluviomarine bottom, on a slope of 3 percent at sea level (When described on August 1, 2014, the soil was wet throughout.)

 Oe--0 to 2 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; strongly acid (pH 5.1); abrupt smooth boundary

 Cg1--2 to 21 cm; silt loam, dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) dry, dark bluish gray (10B 4/1) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 5 percent fine prominent masses of oxidized iron, strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) moist; slightly saline (electrical conductivity of 4 dS/m); slightly effervescent (HCL, 1N); moderately alkaline (pH 7.9); clear smooth boundary

 Cg2--21 to 40 cm; silt loam, 60 percent dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) dry and bluish gray (5B 5/1) moist, 40 percent grayish brown (2.5Y 5/2) dry and dark grayish brown (2.5Y 4/2) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine and fine and many medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 10 percent medium prominent masses of oxidized iron, dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) moist; moderately saline (electrical conductivity of 8 dS/m); slightly effervescent (HCL, 1N); moderately alkaline (pH 7.9); clear smooth boundary

 Cg3--40 to 150 cm; silt loam, 85 percent dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) dry and dark bluish gray (5PB 4/1) moist, 15 percent dark gray (10YR 4/1) dry and black (N 2.5/0) moist; massive; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and nonplastic; common medium roots; few fine and common very fine interstitial pores; moderately saline (electrical conductivity of 8 dS/m); strongly effervescent (HCL, 1N); moderately alkaline (pH 8.3)

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area; latitude 58.4598720, longitude -135.8628640, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
