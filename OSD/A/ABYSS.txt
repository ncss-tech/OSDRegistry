ABYSS SERIES

Landscape--valley of drift plain
Landform--high flood plains
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--sandy alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 1400 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--somewhat poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Sandy, mixed Typic Cryaquents

TYPICAL PEDON: Abyss loam on a high flood plain, on a slopes of 1 percent at sea level (When described on June 6, 2015, the soil was moist to a depth of 30 cm and wet below that depth.)

 C1--0 to 13 cm; loam, light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) moist; 25 percent medium dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6) mottles; massive; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; slightly alkaline (pH 7.5); gradual wavy boundary

 C2--13 to 30 cm; sand, light gray (5Y 7/1) dry, gray (5Y 5/1) moist; massive; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; slightly alkaline (pH 7.6); diffuse irregular boundary

 Cg--30 to 150 cm; sand, dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) dry, very dark gray (N 3/0) moist; massive; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common fine and medium roots throughout; few fine interstitial pores; slightly alkaline (pH 7.7)

TYPE LOCATION: Hoonah-Angoon Census Area, Alaska; latitude 58.4102570, longitude -135.6592080, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
