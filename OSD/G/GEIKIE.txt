GEIKIE SERIES

Landscape--outwash plains
Landform--outwash deltas
Slope--0 to 15 percent
Parent material--outwash
Mean annual precipitation--about 1400 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy over sandy or sandy-skeletal, mixed, superactive Typic Haplocryepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Geikie loam on an outwash delta, on a slope of 1 percent at an elevation of 19 m (When described on August 2, 2014, the soil was moist throughout.)

 A--0 to 7 cm; loam, brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; 5 percent fine strong brown (7.5YR 5/6) mottles; strong medium granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine irregular pores; strongly acid (pH 5.3); clear smooth boundary

 Bw--7 to 32 cm; loam, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) dry, dark olive brown (2.5Y 3/3) moist; moderate fine subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; strongly acid (pH 5.5); clear smooth boundary

 C--32 to 48 cm; silt loam, pale yellow (2.5Y 8/3) dry, light yellowish brown (2.5Y 6/3) moist; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common medium and fine roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; moderately acid (pH 5.6); clear smooth boundary

 2C1--48 to 120 cm; variegated sand; massive; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine and fine irregular pores; 5 percent gravel; strongly effervescent; strongly alkaline (pH 8.5); gradual wavy boundary

 2C2--120 to 150 cm; variegated gravelly sand; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine irregular pores; 25 percent gravel; violently effervescent; strongly alkaline (pH 8.6)

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area; latitude 58.4132791, longitude -135.7280414, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
