TAXONOMIC CLASS: Medial, amorphic, nonacid Typic Cryaquands

TYPICAL PEDON: Grandplateau medial very fine sandy loam on a glacially modified hillslope of 55 percent at an elevation of 109 m (When described on June 03, 2015, the soil was moist throughout.)

 Oa--0 to 2 cm; highly decomposed plant material; slightly acid (pH 6.3); clear smooth boundary

 A--2 to 18 cm; medial very fine sandy loam, 85 percent dark gray (10YR 4/1) dry and black (10YR 2/1) moist, 15 percent light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) dry and dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) moist; weak coarse granular structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, medium, and coarse roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; slightly acid (pH 6.1); clear smooth boundary

 Bw1--18 to 27 cm; medial silt loam, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak coarse subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine and few medium roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 10 percent medium dark brown (7.5YR 3/3) masses of oxidized iron and 10 percent medium dark gray (10YR 4/1) masses of reduced iron; moderately acid (pH 5.7); abrupt irregular boundary

 E--27 to 43 cm; medial silt loam, 60 percent white (2.5Y 8/1) dry and gray (2.5Y 6/1) moist, 40 percent yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) dry and dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) moist; moderate very coarse subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; few very fine and medium roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 10 percent medium dark brown (7.5YR 3/3) masses of oxidized iron; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 Bw2--43 to 120 cm; medial silt loam, brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; moderate very coarse subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; few very fine and fine roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 10 percent medium dark gray (10YR 4/1) masses of reduced iron and 20 percent medium yellowish red (5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; 1 percent gravel, 2 percent stones, and 10 percent cobbles; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 2Bw3--120 to 130 cm; extremely cobbly silt loam, brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak medium granular structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; 2 percent fine dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) masses of reduced iron and 3 percent fine dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron; 5 percent gravel, 20 percent stones, and 40 percent cobbles; moderately acid (pH 5.6); clear smooth boundary

 3R--130 cm; bedrock
