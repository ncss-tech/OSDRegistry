GLACIERBAY SERIES

Landscape--outwash plains
Landform--outwash deltas
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--glaciolacustrine deposits
Mean annual precipitation--about 1400 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--very poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Fine-silty over sandy or sandy-skeletal, mixed, superactive, calcareous Typic Cryaquents

TYPICAL PEDON: Glacierbay silt loam on an outwash delta, on a slope of 1 percent at an elevation of 19 m (When described on August 3, 2014, the soil was wet throughout.)

 A--0 to 8 cm; silt loam, grayish brown (2.5Y 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (2.5Y 3/2) moist; moderate fine granular structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; common very fine and fine irregular pores; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 Cg1--8 to 25 cm; silt loam, gray (10YR 5/1) dry, dark gray (N 4/0) wet; weak medium subangular blocky structure; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 30 percent fine dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) masses of oxidized iron; moderately acid (pH 5.8); gradual smooth boundary

 Cg2--25 to 58 cm; silt loam, dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) dry, gray (N 5/0) wet; weak medium subangular blocky structure; moderately hard, firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common medium and fine roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 5 percent fine strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; slightly effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 7.9); clear wavy boundary

 2Cg1--58 to 100 cm; sand, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, gray (5Y 5/1) wet; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine irregular pores; strongly effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.1); gradual wavy boundary

 2Cg2--100 to 130 cm; fine sand, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, gray (5Y 5/1) wet; single grain; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; 5 percent yellowish brown (10YR 5/6) masses of oxidized iron; few fine irregular pores; strongly effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.1); diffuse wavy boundary

 2Cg3--130 to 150 cm; sand, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, gray (5Y 5/1) wet; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; 7 percent strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron; few very fine irregular pores; violently effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.3)
