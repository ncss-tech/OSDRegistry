TYPICAL PEDON: Grassylake stratified channery loamy sand on an east facing linear, convex berm on a slope of 2 percent at an elevation of 41 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on July 12, 2015.)

 Oe--0 to 2 cm; moderately decomposed plant material, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; many very fine and coarse, common medium, and few fine roots; 3 percent channers; moderately acid (pH 6.0); clear smooth boundary

 AC--2 to 12 cm; stratified channery loamy sand, 75 percent dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) and 25 percent brown (7.5YR 4/2) dry, 75 percent very dark brown (10YR 2/2) and 25 percent very dark brown (7.5YR 2.5/2) moist; weak medium granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine and fine and few medium roots; common fine irregular pores; 20 percent channers; strongly acid (pH 5.4); clear smooth boundary

 C1--12 to 30 cm; extremely channery sand, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and fine, and common medium roots; 68 percent channers; moderately acid (pH 6.0); clear smooth boundary

 C2--30 to 60 cm; very channery sand, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine and few fine roots; 53 percent channers; slightly acid (pH 6.2); gradual smooth boundary

 C3--60 to 150 cm; very channery sand, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky, nonplastic; few very fine roots; 53 percent channers; slightly acid (pH 6.2)
