FINGERS SERIES

Landscape--outwash plains
Landform--drainageways of glaciated plains
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 1400 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--somewhat poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--aquic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Sandy-skeletal, mixed Oxyaquic Cryorthents

TYPICAL PEDON: Fingers loamy coarse sand in a drainageway of a glaciated plain, on a slope of 2 percent at an elevation of 22 m (When described on June 6, 2013, the soil was moist from the surface to a depth of 43 cm and wet from a depth of 43 to 150 cm.)

 Oi--0 to 2 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; neutral (pH 6.9); abrupt smooth boundary

 A--2 to 8 cm; very gravelly loamy coarse sand, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; weak granular structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 2 percent stones, 5 percent cobbles, 10 percent medium gravel, and 25 percent fine gravel; very slightly effervescent; slightly alkaline (pH 7.4); clear smooth boundary

 C1--8 to 43 cm; variegated extremely gravelly coarse sand; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots throughout; common very fine and fine interstitial pores; 2 percent stones, 10 percent cobbles, 25 percent medium gravel, and 25 percent fine gravel; noneffervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.3); gradual smooth boundary

 C2--43 to 150 cm; variegated extremely gravelly coarse sand; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; 4 percent stones, 10 percent fine gravel, 10 percent cobbles, and 50 percent medium gravel; noneffervescent; strongly alkaline (pH 8.5)

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area, Alaska; latitude 58.6572803, longitude -136.4027196, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
