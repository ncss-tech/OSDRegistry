 C3--95 to 150 cm; variegated extremely gravelly coarse sand; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine interstitial pores; 5 percent cobbles and 70 percent medium gravel; strongly effervescent (HCL, 1N); strongly alkaline (pH 8.5)
