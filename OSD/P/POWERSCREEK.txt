TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, mixed, superactive, acid, subgelic Turbic Humigelepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Powerscreek sandy loam on a linear, convex rise, on a slope of 0 percent at an elevation of 25 m (The soil was moist from 0 to 140 cm and wet, non-satiated, from 140 to 150 cm when described on August 28, 2019.)

 Oe--0 to 6 cm; moderately decomposed plant material, dark gray (10YR 4/1) dry, black (10YR 2/1) moist; many very fine, fine, and coarse and few medium roots throughout; extremely acid (pH 4.2); clear smooth boundary

 A--6 to 12 cm; sandy loam, dark gray (7.5YR 4/1) dry, black (7.5YR 2.5/1) moist; weak coarse granular structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; many very fine and few fine roots throughout; extremely acid (pH 4.1); clear irregular boundary

 Bhsjj--12 to 43 cm; sandy loam, 70 percent reddish brown (2.5YR 4/3) and 30 percent dark reddish gray (2.5YR 4/1) dry, 70 percent dark reddish brown (2.5YR 2.5/3) and 30 percent reddish black (2.5YR 2.5/1) moist; weak coarse subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; many very fine and common fine roots throughout; very strongly acid (pH 4.9); abrupt irregular boundary

 BC--43 to 110 cm; fine sandy loam, 90 percent brown (10YR 5/3) and 10 percent light brownish gray (10YR 6/2) dry, 90 percent dark brown (10YR 3/3) and 10 percent dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; common very fine roots throughout; very fine tubular pores; very strongly acid (pH 4.6); clear smooth boundary

 C--110 to 140 cm; loamy sand, yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; common very fine roots throughout; very fine irregular pores; very strongly acid (pH 4.7); clear smooth boundary

 Cg--140 to 150 cm; very fine sandy loam, light gray (2.5Y 7/2) dry, grayish brown (2.5Y 5/2) moist; massive; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; nonsmeary; very fine tubular pores; 1 percent fine cylindrical dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) iron-manganese nodules and 20 percent medium irregular dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron; very strongly acid (pH 4.8)
