TRAVANS SERIES

Landscape--ridges, hillslopes
Landform--hills
Slope--2 to 60 percent
Parent material--loess-influenced colluvium over rhyolite
Mean annual precipitation--about 250 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--moderately deep
Drainage class--well drained
Soil moisture regime--aridic bordering on xeric
Soil temperature regime--frigid

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Loamy-skeletal, mixed, superactive, frigid Xeric Haplocalcids

TYPICAL PEDON: Travans gravelly loam on an 11-percent slope in an area of rangeland at an elevation of 1554 m

 A--0 to 13 cm; gravelly loam, brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and slightly plastic; common very fine and fine roots; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 20 percent gravel; finely disseminated calcium carbonate (5 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); strongly effervescent; slightly alkaline (pH 7.8); clear smooth boundary

 Bk1--13 to 28 cm; gravelly loam, pale brown (10YR 6/3) dry, brown (10YR 4/3) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common very fine and fine roots; common very fine and fine tubular pores; 20 percent gravel; finely disseminated calcium carbonate (15 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); violently effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); clear smooth boundary

 Bk2--28 to 53 cm; very gravelly loam, very pale brown (10YR 7/3) dry, yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and slightly plastic; common very fine and fine roots; common very fine and fine tubular pores; 35 percent gravel; finely disseminated calcium carbonate (25 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); calcium carbonate coatings 2 to 5 mm thick on bottom of rock fragments; violently effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); gradual smooth boundary

 Bk3--53 to 76 cm; extremely gravelly sandy loam, very pale brown (10YR 8/3) dry, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) moist; weak fine subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; few very fine and fine roots; few very fine and fine tubular pores; 30 percent gravel, 20 percent channers, and 10 percent flagstones; finely disseminated calcium carbonate (30 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); calcium carbonate coatings 2 to 5 mm thick on bottom of rock fragments; violently effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); abrupt smooth boundary

 R--76 cm; fractured rhyolite
