TYPICAL PEDON:  Thosand silty clay in an area of pastureland at an elevation of 1914 m (Colors are for moist soil unless otherwise noted.)

 Oi--0 to 13 cm; slightly decomposed organic material; strongly effervescent

 Akg--13 to 25 cm; gray (5Y 5/1) silty clay, light gray (5Y 7/1) dry; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; hard, friable, moderately sticky and moderately plastic; many very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots; many very fine and fine tubular pores; finely disseminated calcium carbonate; violently effervescent (70 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.4); gradual smooth boundary

 Bkg1--25 to 43 cm; gray (5Y 5/1) silty clay, gray (5Y 6/1) dry; moderate fine subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, moderately sticky and moderately plastic; many very fine, fine, medium, and coarse roots; many very fine and fine tubular and irregular pores; finely disseminated calcium carbonate; violently effervescent; (70 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.1); clear wavy boundary

 Bkg2--43 to 69 cm; dark greenish gray (5GY 4/1) silty clay, pale olive (5Y 6/3) dry; common medium and coarse prominent dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6) iron concentrations and common medium and coarse distinct black (5Y 2.5/2) iron depletions; moderate coarse subangular blocky structure; hard, friable, moderately sticky and moderately plastic; common medium roots; many very fine and fine tubular and irregular pores; 10 percent gravel; finely disseminated calcium carbonate; violently effervescent (55 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.0); clear wavy boundary

 Bkg3--69 to 112 cm; greenish gray (5GY 5/1) gravelly loam, light olive gray (5Y 6/2) dry; many medium and coarse prominent dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/6) iron concentrations and common medium and coarse distinct dark bluish gray (5B 4/1) iron depletions; moderate coarse subangular blocky structure; hard, friable, moderately sticky and moderately plastic; few coarse roots; many very fine and fine tubular and irregular pores; 30 percent gravel; slightly effervescent (1 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.2); clear wavy boundary

 Bkg4--112 to 125 cm; olive gray (5Y 4/2) very gravelly silty clay loam, pale olive (5Y 6/3) dry; moderate coarse subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common medium roots; many very fine and fine tubular and irregular pores; 55 percent gravel; slightly effervescent (1 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.2); clear wavy boundary

 2Cg--125 to 165 cm; dark gray (5Y 4/1) extremely gravelly loamy coarse sand, gray (5Y 6/1) dry; massive; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, and medium tubular and interstitial pores; 65 percent gravel; slightly effervescent; (1 percent calcium carbonate equivalent); moderately alkaline (pH 8.1)
