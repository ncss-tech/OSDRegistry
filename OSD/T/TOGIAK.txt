TAXONOMIC CLASS: Sandy-skeletal, mixed Typic Cryofluvents

TYPICAL PEDON: Togiak very gravelly coarse sand on a southeast-facing, convex berm, on a slope of 3 percent at an elevation of 4 m (When described on July 25, 2013, the soil was dry to a depth of 35 cm and moist throughout the rest of the profile.)

 Oi--0 to 4 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; many medium and common very fine roots; neutral (pH 7.0)

 C1--4 to 47 cm; very gravelly coarse sand, gray (10YR 6/1) dry, very dark gray (10YR 3/1) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; many very fine to medium roots; 50 percent gravel; neutral (pH 7.0); gradual smooth boundary

 C2--47 to 150 cm; very gravelly coarse sand, gray (10YR 6/1) dry, very dark gray (10YR 3/1) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; 50 percent gravel; slightly alkaline (pH 7.4)
