TOPSY SERIES

Landscape--mountain valleys
Landform--pitted outwash plains
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--glaciolacustrine deposits over outwash
Mean annual precipitation--about 1400 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 5 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Sandy, mixed Typic Cryaquents

TYPICAL PEDON: Topsy very fine sandy loam, forested, on an outwash plain, on a slope of 3 percent at an elevation of 12 m (When described on June 19, 2015, the soil was moist from the surface to a depth of 14 cm and wet from a depth of 14 to 150 cm.)

 Oi--0 to 3 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; neutral (pH 6.7); clear smooth boundary

 A--3 to 14 cm; very fine sandy loam, light brownish gray (2.5Y 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (2.5Y 4/2) moist; moderate medium granular structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine, fine, coarse, and very coarse and many medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine irregular pores; noneffervescent; slightly acid (pH 6.3); clear smooth boundary

 Cg1--14 to 27 cm; silty clay loam, gray (2.5Y 6/1) dry, dark gray (2.5Y 4/1) moist; massive; slightly hard, friable, moderately sticky and moderately plastic; common very fine, fine, and medium roots throughout; common very fine and fine irregular pores; 15 percent fine masses of oxidized iron, olive (5Y 5/6) moist, and 20 percent medium masses of reduced iron, gray (5Y 5/1) moist; noneffervescent; neutral (pH 6.6); abrupt smooth boundary

 2Cg2--27 to 150 cm; sand, light gray (5Y 7/1) dry, gray (5Y 5/1) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; many very fine and common fine interstitial pores; slightly effervescent (HCL, 1N); neutral (pH 7.0)

TYPE LOCATION: Glacier Bay National Park and Preserve-Gustavus Area, Alaska; Hoonah-Angoon Census Area; latitude 58.4202200, longitude -135.7586110, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined by a GPS unit)
