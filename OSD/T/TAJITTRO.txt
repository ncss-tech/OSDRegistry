TAJITTRO SERIES

Landscape--alluvial plains
Landform--stream terraces
Slope--0 to 3 percent
Parent material--alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 255 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -6 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--moderately well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--gelic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-silty, mixed, superactive, nonacid, subgelic Aquic Haploturbels

TYPICAL PEDON: Tajittro silt loam on a forested terrace with a slope of 2 percent and an elevation of 128 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on July 11, 2014.)

 Oi--0 to 6 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; common medium roots; slightly alkaline (pH 7.4); abrupt smooth boundary

 Oe--6 to 11 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; common medium and coarse roots; moderately acid (pH 5.8); abrupt smooth boundary

 A--11 to 18 cm; silt loam; brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; weak fine granular structure; soft, very friable; common fine and medium roots; common fine and medium pores; noneffervescent; slightly alkaline (pH 7.6); clear wavy boundary

 Bw--18 to 28 cm; silt loam; light brownish gray (2.5Y 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (2.5Y 4/2) moist; weak medium subangular blocky structure; soft, very friable; common fine and medium roots; few fine and medium pores; 5 percent light olive brown (2.5Y 5/6) moist, mottles in matrix; strongly effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.3); clear smooth boundary

 Bwjj--28 to 75 cm; silt loam; 50 percent light yellowish brown (2.5Y 6/3) dry, olive brown (2.5Y 4/3) moist, and 50 percent light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) moist; moderate thin platy structure; slightly hard, friable; common fine roots; few fine and medium pores; 10 percent light olive brown (2.5Y 5/6) moist, masses of oxidized iron in matrix; violently effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.3); clear smooth boundary

 Cf--75 to 100 cm; stratified permanently frozen very fine sandy loam to permanently frozen silt loam; light yellowish brown (2.5Y 6/3) dry, olive brown (2.5Y 4/3) moist; weak thin platy structure; 20 percent light olive brown (2.5Y 5/6) moist, masses of oxidized iron in matrix; very slightly effervescent; moderately alkaline (pH 8.0)

TYPE LOCATION: Yukon-Koyukuk Census Area, Alaska; latitude 66.2784700 degrees, longitude -148.0753589 degrees, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
