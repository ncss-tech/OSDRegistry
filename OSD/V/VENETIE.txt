VENETIE SERIES

Landscape--alluvial plains
Landform--stream terraces, flood plains
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 255 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -6 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--somewhat excessively drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--ustic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Sandy-skeletal, mixed Typic Cryorthents

TYPICAL PEDON: Venetie gravelly fine sandy loam on a woodland terrace with a slope of 3 percent and an elevation of 190 m (The soil was moist throughout when described on July 4, 2012.)

 Oe--0 to 4 cm; moderately decomposed plant material; many fine roots and common medium roots; slightly acid (pH 6.5); clear smooth boundary

 A--4 to 16 cm; gravelly fine sandy loam; 90 percent light gray (10YR 7/2) dry, grayish brown (10YR 5/2) moist, and 10 percent light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4)dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) moist; moderate medium granular structure; soft, very friable; few fine roots and common medium roots; common fine and medium pores; 20 percent gravel; noneffervescent; neutral (pH 7.1); clear smooth boundary

 C1--16 to 55 cm; very gravelly loamy sand; light gray (10YR 7/1) dry, gray (10YR 5/1) moist; single grain; loose; common fine roots; few fine and medium pores; 40 percent gravel; noneffervescent; neutral (pH 7.3); gradual wavy boundary

 C2--55 to 90 cm; very gravelly fine sand; light gray (10YR 7/1) dry, gray (10YR 5/1) moist; single grain; loose; common fine roots; few fine and medium pores; 40 percent gravel; noneffervescent; neutral (pH 7.3); gradual wavy boundary

 C3--90 to 150 cm; very gravelly fine sand; light gray (10YR 7/1) dry, gray (10YR 5/1) moist; single grain; loose; 55 percent gravel; noneffervescent; slightly alkaline (pH 7.7)

TYPE LOCATION: Yukon-Koyukuk Census Area, Alaska; latitude 66.9632222 degrees, longitude -146.6654167 degrees, datum WGS84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
