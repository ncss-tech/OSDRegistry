ROCKY SERIES

Landscape--mountain valleys, outwash plains
Landform--outwash terraces, earth hummocks on convex slopes of patterned ground 
Slope--0 to 5 percent
Parent material--volcanic ash over outwash
Mean annual precipitation--about 1000 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about 1 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class-- well drained
Soil moisture regime--udic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Medial over sandy or sandy-skeletal, amorphic over mixed Typic Haplocryands

TYPICAL PEDON: Rocky medial sandy loam on a nearly level, linear glaciated plain, on a slope of 1 percent at an elevation of 57 m (The soil was moist throughout the profile when described on August 4, 2013.)

 Oi--0 to 9 cm; slightly decomposed plant material; many very fine and common fine to coarse roots; very strongly acid (pH 4.6); clear smooth boundary

 A--9 to 50 cm; medial sandy loam, yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 3/4) moist; weak medium granular structure; soft, very friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; common very fine to medium roots; 3 percent cobbles; strongly acid (pH 5.5); clear smooth boundary

 Bw--50 to 90 cm; medial silt loam, yellow (10YR 7/6) dry, yellowish brown (10YR 5/6) moist; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; 8 percent medium irregular masses of oxidized iron, yellowish red (5YR 5/6) moist, clear boundaries in matrix; 3 percent cobbles; slightly acid (pH 6.1); clear smooth boundary

 2C--90 to 150 cm; extremely gravelly coarse sand, gray (10YR 5/1) dry, very dark gray (10YR 3/1) moist; single grain; loose, nonsticky and nonplastic; 60 percent gravel and 10 percent cobbles; slightly acid (pH 6.3)

TYPE LOCATION: Dillingham Census Area, Alaska, about 35 km west of the village of Togiak; latitude 59.0574408 degrees north, longitude 159.7413647 degrees west, datum WGS 84 (coordinates determined with a GPS)
