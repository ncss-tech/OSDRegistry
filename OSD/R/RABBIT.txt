RABBIT SERIES

Landscape--valleys
Landform--floodplain
Slope--0 to 8 percent
Parent material--coarse-loamy alluvium over gravelly alluvium
Mean annual precipitation--about 500 mm
Mean annual air temperature--about -3 degrees C
Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--somewhat poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic
Soil temperature regime--cryic
Soil moisture subclass--typic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, mixed, superactive, nonacid Fluvaquentic Cryaquepts

TYPICAL PEDON: Rabbit silt on a southwest facing concave, linear toeslope on a slope of 2 percent at an elevation of 316 m (The soil was wet, satiated throughout the soil profile when described on June 26, 2018.)

 Oe--0 to 6 cm; gravelly mucky peat, dark gray (10YR 4/1) dry, black (10YR 2/1) moist; many very fine and common fine roots; moderately acid (pH 5.9); clear smooth boundary.

 Ag--6 to 33 cm; mucky silt, light olive brown (2.5Y 5/3) dry, dark olive brown (2.5Y 3/3) moist; weak fine granular structure; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; many very fine and common fine, medium, and coarse roots; 1 percent dark reddish brown (5YR 2.5/2) masses of oxidized iron in matrix and 4 percent dark reddish brown (5YR 3/4) masses of oxidized iron on surfaces along pores; 6 percent gravel; moderately acid (pH 5.7); gradual wavy boundary

 Bg--33 to 61 cm; silt loam, light brownish gray (2.5Y 6/2) dry, dark grayish brown (2.5Y 4/2) moist; 7 percent brown (7.5YR 4/4) mottles; weak very fine subangular blocky structure; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine and medium, and many fine roots; 2 percent strong brown (7.5YR 4/6) masses of oxidized iron on surfaces along pores; 1 percent gravel; moderately acid (pH 6.0); abrupt smooth boundary

 Cg1--61 to 67 cm; very gravelly loam, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, dark gray (5Y 4/1) moist; massive; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; common very fine and fine roots; 40 percent gravel; slightly acid (pH 6.2); abrupt smooth boundary

 Cg2--67 to 86 cm; silt loam, gray (N 5/), dry, very dark gray (N 3/), moist; massive; friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; moderately acid (pH 6.0); abrupt smooth boundary

 Cg3--86 to 150 cm; very gravelly loam, gray (5Y 6/1) dry, dark gray (5Y 4/1) moist; massive; very friable, nonsticky, nonplastic; 45 percent gravel; moderately acid (pH 6.0)
