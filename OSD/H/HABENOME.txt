HABENOME SERIES

Landscape--mountains
Landform--basins and benches on glaciated mountain slopes
Slope--0 to 30 percent
Parent material--alluvium, lacustrine deposits, and till derived from granite
Mean annual precipitation--about 990 mm

Depth class--very deep
Drainage class--poorly drained
Soil moisture regime--aquic

TAXONOMIC CLASS: Coarse-loamy, mixed, superactive Typic Cryaquolls

TYPICAL PEDON: Habenome silt loam in a wet meadow, on a 7-percent, northeast-facing slope at an elevation of 2301 m

 A--0 to 28 cm; silt loam, brown (10YR 5/3) dry, dark brown (7.5YR 3/3) moist; weak fine and medium subangular blocky structure; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; common fine and few medium roots; few fine tubular pores; strongly acid (pH 5.5); clear smooth boundary

 AC--28 to 36 cm; sandy loam, yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; many coarse prominent strong brown (7.5YR 5/6) redoximorphic concentrations; single grain; slightly hard, very friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few fine and medium roots; few very fine irregular pores; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 C--36 to 43 cm; silt loam, light yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) dry, dark yellowish brown (10YR 4/4) moist; common medium distinct light gray (7.5YR 7/1) redoximorphic depletions; massive; slightly hard, friable, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few fine and medium roots; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 Ab--43 to 56 cm; silt loam, brown (10YR 4/3) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; massive; hard, firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few fine and medium roots; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 C1b--56 to 71 cm; sandy loam, brown (7.5YR 5/4) dry, dark brown (7.5YR 3/4) moist; many coarse prominent yellowish red (5YR 5/6) redoximorphic concentrations; single grain; slightly hard, very friable, nonsticky and nonplastic; few fine and medium roots; few very fine irregular pores; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 C2b--71 to 89 cm; silt loam, brown (7.5YR 4/3) dry, dark brown (10YR 3/3) moist; many coarse prominent light gray (5Y 7/1) redoximorphic depletions; massive; hard, very firm, slightly sticky and slightly plastic; few fine roots; moderately acid (pH 5.7); clear smooth boundary

 Ab--89 to 114 cm; silt loam, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) dry, very dark brown (10YR 2/2) moist; massive grading to weak medium subangular blocky structure; hard, friable, slightly sticky and nonplastic; few fine roots; common fine irregular pores; 10 percent partially decomposed wood fragments; sulfur odor; moderately acid (pH 5.7); gradual wavy boundary

 Cb--114 to 155 cm; silt loam, dark grayish brown (10YR 4/2) dry, very dark grayish brown (10YR 3/2) moist; massive; hard, firm, slightly sticky and nonplastic; few fine roots; moderately acid (pH 5.7)
